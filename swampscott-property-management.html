Swampscott Property Management FAQ

What types of properties do you manage in Swampscott?

We manage single-family homes, condos, small multifamily buildings, and house-hack style setups where the owner may live in one unit and rent the others. If you are not sure whether your property is a fit, reach out and we can talk through it.

How much does property management cost in Swampscott?

Most owners pay a percentage of collected rent plus any agreed-upon leasing fees. Pricing is straightforward and transparent so you can see exactly what you are paying for. After we know more about your property and needs, we will provide a written quote.

Can you help if I already have a tenant in place?

Yes. We can take over mid-lease, review your current lease terms, and work with your resident so the transition is smooth. We will discuss timing together so there is minimal disruption.

Do you work with Section 8 and other voucher programs?

We do. We are familiar with inspections, paperwork, and communication with local housing authorities and can help you stay organized and compliant.
