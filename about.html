Meet the Founder

Anthony Velardi is a licensed real estate professional, property manager, and founder of AV Realty & Management. With years of hands-on experience managing multifamily assets, advising investors, and overseeing operations for large residential communities, Anthony launched AV Realty & Management with one goal: to deliver real estate solutions grounded in integrity, strategy, and service.

Growing up in housing environments where stability wasn’t guaranteed, Anthony developed a deep understanding of how impactful real estate can be in people’s lives. Today, he brings that insight into every transaction — blending technical knowledge with a client-first approach that helps buyers, sellers, and investors move with clarity and confidence.

Whether you're a landlord seeking peace of mind, a first-time buyer navigating your next move, or an investor focused on long-term growth, Anthony is committed to providing the guidance and accountability you deserve. AV Realty & Management was founded on the belief that real estate should empower — and every client should feel seen, supported, and served.
