About AV Realty & Management

Founded by Anthony Velardi, AV Realty & Management was built on a passion for helping people and a deep understanding of what it takes to manage property with integrity.

As someone who grew up in housing instability and now owns multiple properties, Anthony brings a rare perspective: he knows what it’s like to be both the tenant and the owner.

That’s why his company is driven by purpose — to provide honest service, protect your investment, and help families find homes they can trust.

Whether you're a first-time buyer, seasoned investor, or landlord looking for peace of mind, you're in the right hands.
