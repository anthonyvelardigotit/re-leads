Spotlight: Key North Shore Real Estate Markets

Swampscott

Coastal living with commuter access into Boston. Swampscott offers single-family homes, multi-family options, and strong long-term demand from buyers who want to be near the ocean without sacrificing convenience.

Salem

Historic charm, a vibrant downtown, and consistent rental demand make Salem a favorite for both homeowners and investors. Condos, multi-families, and unique historic properties create a wide range of opportunities.

Beverly

A balanced market with coastal neighborhoods, downtown living, and suburban streets. Beverly is a strong choice for buyers seeking community feel, train access, and long-term stability.

Lynn

One of the most dynamic and evolving North Shore markets. Lynn offers value relative to other coastal towns, strong rental demand, and opportunities for both first-time buyers and investors.
